About Temperature Converter

This website provides a simple tool to convert temperatures between Celsius (°C) and Fahrenheit (°F).

Conversion Example

Convert 20°C to Fahrenheit:

20°C is equal to 68°F.

How to Use:

  1. Enter the temperature in the input field.
  2. Select the unit of the temperature (Celsius or Fahrenheit).
  3. Click the Convert button.
  4. The converted temperature will be displayed below.
